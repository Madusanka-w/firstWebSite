Annual Medical Plan



  • What is an Annual Plan:

    • Covering a range of health insurance needs at a highly affordable price, you can choose your best suited Annual Medical Plan from 5 packages based on your financial investment. It is an annually renewable health insurance policy that comes with a highly advantageous ‘No Claim Bonus’ that increases the basic sum assured by 5% up to a maximum of 50% for every claim-free year.



  • Benefits:

    • No Claim Bonus - A No Claim Bonus that increases the basic sum assured by 5% up to a maximum of 50% for every claim-free year.
    Hospitalization Benefits - Hospitalization benefits inclusive of daily allowance, medicine charges and maternity benefits for government hospitals.
    Entitled for claim in 30 days - Entitled for claim in 30 days after policy commencement.
    Additional Benefits - Benefits applicable for treatments for cataract, hernia, hysterectomy, all types of cardiac surgeries, cancer treatments, and more.
    Surgery expenses - Day care surgery expenses.
    Donor expenses - Organ donor expenses.

Surgical Medical Plan



  • What is a Surgical Plan:

    • For financial coverage in the event of an unexpected surgery, the IWT Insurance Surgery Plan provides you with a comprehensive policy that covers surgery costs up to a pre-specified maximum limit. The policy covers you, your spouse and 5 dependent children, and must be purchased together with an IWT Insurance health insurance policy.



  • Benefits:

    • Maximum of LKR 1,000,000 - Maximum of LKR 1,000,000 in the event of a surgery performed under medical necessity, or a pre-specified percentage from the maximum limit depending on the nature of the surgery and selected benefits.
    IWT Insurance Surgical Plan - Under the IWT Insurance Surgical Plan you can claim a percentage of your IWT Insurance surgical Plan sum assured between 10% - 100% depending on the type of surgery.
    360 plus listed surgeries - More than 360 listed surgeries and many non- listed surgeries are covered.
    Up to five times of the insured value - You get up to five times of the insured value for the total policy period.
    Surgeries performed abroad -Valid for surgeries done in Singapore, India, Malaysia and Sri Lanka.
    Cover period - Cover period from 5 - 30 years.
