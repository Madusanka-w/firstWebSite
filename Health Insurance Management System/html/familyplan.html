Annual Medical Plans


  • What is an annual plan:

    • You can select the best fit Annual Medical Plan from 5 packages based on the financial investment, covering a variety of health insurance requirements at a highly competitive price. It is an annual renewable health insurance policy that comes with a highly attractive 'No Claim Incentive' that raises the guaranteed basic amount by 5 percent for each claim-free year to a maximum of 50 percent.


  • Benifits:

    • No Claim Bonus - A No Claim Bonus that raises the guaranteed basic amount by 5 percent for each claim-free year to a maximum of 50 percent.


    Hospitalization Benifits - Hospitalization incentives for private hospitals, including room charges, medical professional fees, theater charges, ambulance charges, maternity benefits, etc.


    Additional Benifits - The advantages refer to the treatment of cataracts, hernia, hysterectomy, all forms of heart surgery, treatment of cancer and more.


    Surgery expenses - Day care surgery expenses.


    Donor expenses - Organ donor expenses.


    Entitled for claim in 30 days - Entitled for claim in 30 days after policy commencement.


  • Terms and conditions:

  • The policy can be obtained by adults between the ages of 18 to 60 years with the cover ceasing at the age of 69.
  • The policy can be obtained for children between the ages of 180 days to 17 years with the cover ceasing at the age of 21.

Hospital Cash


  • What is a Hospital Cash plan:

    • A unique and creative insurance package that, regardless of the actual bills or expenditures, offers a fixed sum as cover for any day you are hospitalized. This affords the policyholder with the fixed daily cash benefit a blanket cover for additional expenses.


  • Benifits

    • LKR 12,500 on a daily basis - Up to LKR 12,500 on a daily basic to cover medical expenses.


    Double the cash benefit - Double the cash benefit of LKR 25,000 if submitted to the ICU.


    4 days' cash benefit - Added 4 days' cash benefit if hospital stay extends beyond 10 days.


    Up to 5 times the annual limit - Up to 5 times the annual limit for the total policy period.


  • Terms and conditions:

  • The policy can be obtained by adults between the ages of 18 to 60 years with the cover ceasing at the age of 70, while the children need to be under the age of 3 months to 20 years, and they will be covered until the age of 25 years.
  • Must be obtained with a IWT life insurance policy.
